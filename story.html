The Enormous Turnip

Once there lived an old man. He was very fond of turnips, so he decided to grow some turnips seeds. All the turnips grew and grew, but one turnip grew enormous. The old man wanted it for his tea. So he pulled and he pulled but he couldn't pull it up. So the old man called the old woman to help him pull the enormous turnip. Then they pulled and they pulled but they couldn't pull it up. So the old woman called the boy to help them pull the enormous turnip. Then they pulled and they pulled but they couldn't pull it up. So the boy called the girl to help them pull the enormous turnip. Then they pulled and they pulled but they couldn't pull it up. So the girl called the dog to help them pull the enormous turnip. Then they pulled and they pulled and up popped the enormous turnip! And then they all had turnip for tea.


Goldilocks and the Three Bears

Once upon a time there were three bears. Daddy bear, Mummy bear and Baby bear. The three bears loved to eat porridge and one morning they made porridge for breakfast and went for a walk. Soon a girl named Goldielocks passed by.She saw the bear's house. She was very hungry and decided to go in the house. She noticed three bowls of porridge. She ate from the first one and said that it was too hot. Then she ate from the second one and said that it was too cold. Then she ate from the third bowl and said that it was just right and finished it. Then she saw three chairs. She sat on the first one and said that it was too hard. Then she sat on the second one and said that it was too soft. Then she sat on the third one and said that it was just right. But suddenly it broke and so she went upstairs. She saw three beds. She slept on the first one and said that it was too hard. Then she slept on the second one and said that it was too soft. Then she slept on the third one and said that it was just right and she went to sleep. After some time the bears came back. They saw that someone had eaten their porridge and finished Baby bear's. Then they saw that someone had sat in their chairs and had broken Baby bear's. Then they saw that someone had slept on their beds and that person was sleeping in Baby bears bed! Just then Goldielocks woke up and as soon as she saw the three angry bears, she fled and was never seen again in the forest.


The Magic Porridge Pot

Once upon a time there lived a little girl with her mother. One day she met an old woman who gave her a magic porridge pot. She told her that if you said "Cook,little pot,cook" then the little pot would cook porridge and if you said "Stop,little pot,stop" then the little pot will stop cooking. The little girl happily took the pot to her mother and told her how it works. Then the girl left as she had to go to another town. The mother decided to make some porridge and said "Cook,little pot,cook" and it started making porridge and the mother went to sleep. Soon the kitchen was full of porridge and the magic pot went on cooking. Soon the house was full of porridge and the magic pot went on cooking. Soon the street was full of porridge and the magic pot went on cooking. Soon the whole town was full of porridge and the magic pot still went on cooking. Just then the little girl came back and shouted "Stop,little pot,stop" and at last the little pot stopped cooking and the mother woke up. But till now the whole village is still eating porridge!!
